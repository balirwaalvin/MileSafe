Understanding traffic signs and rules is essential for safe driving and avoiding fines or accidents.

Common Traffic Rules

  • Speed Limits: Always obey posted speed limits. Adjust your speed for weather and traffic conditions.
  • Seatbelt Use: Buckle up every time, no matter how short the trip.
  • No Phone Use: Avoid texting or calling while driving. Use hands-free only if necessary.
  • Right-of-Way: Know when to yield to other vehicles and pedestrians.
  • Lane Discipline: Stay in your lane and use indicators for turns and lane changes.

Key Road Signs

  • Stop Sign: Come to a complete stop and proceed when safe.
  • Yield Sign: Give way to other vehicles.
  • Speed Limit Sign: Indicates the maximum speed allowed.
  • No Entry Sign: Do not enter the road or area.
  • Pedestrian Crossing: Watch for people crossing the road.
  • School Zone: Slow down and watch for children.
