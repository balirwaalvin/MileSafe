What to Do in an Accident

  • Stay Calm: Take a deep breath and assess the situation. Panic can make things worse.
  • Check for Injuries: Make sure everyone is safe. Call emergency services (999) or use the SOS button if needed.
  • Move to Safety: If possible, move vehicles out of traffic. Turn on hazard lights.
  • Exchange Information: Share names, contact info, and insurance details with other parties.
  • Document the Scene: Take photos and notes for insurance and police reports.
